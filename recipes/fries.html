Fries

Description

French fries are a popular food made by deep-frying potato strips. They are typically served hot, often salted, and can be enjoyed as a snack or a side dish with various condiments like ketchup, mayonnaise, or vinegar. French fries come in various shapes and sizes, including shoestring, crinkle-cut, and curly, and can be found in fast food restaurants and cafes worldwide

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • oil
  • salt

Recipe

  1. cut the potatoes
  2. soak and dry the potatoes
  3. fry in oil
  4. drain before serving in plate
